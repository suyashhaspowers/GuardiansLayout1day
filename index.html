OUR MISSION

Our mission is to raise awareness for animal endangerment. We will try to influence the world that we must help and become active with the current animal crisis. With the help of an inspired society we can end the wildlife crisis and begin the journey to a world of peace and harmony.

ABOUT US

Guardians Of the Grove is a non-profit organization with the desired goal of raising awareness towards the endangerment of animals. We will try all we can to invoke a change in the ongoing animal crisis.
