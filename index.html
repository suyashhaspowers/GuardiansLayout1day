OUR MISSION

Our goal is to raise awareness for animal endangerment.
We will try to influence the world that we must help
and become active with the current animal crisis.
With your help this can be achieved.

About Us

Founded in Waterloo in the year of 2015, Guardians of the Grove is a non-profit organization with the desired goal of raising awareness towards the endangerment of animals. We follow the same ambition as other organizations such as WWF, FWS and more. We will try all we can to invoke a change in the ongoing animal crisis and preserve a birght future for our future generations to come. With the help of an inspired world we can make our aspiration a reality.
