Black Rino

Black rhinos live throughout southern and eastern Africa.They tend to look for a habitat with a nearby water source with a healthy supply of plant life like shrubs and woody herb, they have a darkish grey colour and two horns which grow throughout their life from the skin at heir base.Black rings can live up to 45 years and weigh to 1,350 kg for males and up to 900 kg for females. There main threat comes from horn poaching for the illegal wild life trade market they have been a huge demand for some asian consumers, specifically in Vietnam for medicine and as a symbol of wealth.

Mountin Gorilla

Mountain gorillas live in deep in the forests of the mountain as high as 8,000 to 1300 feet located in the Virunga mountains in central Africa, they have thick hair that helps them survive in freezing temperature habitats. There main threat comes from habitat loss having to move farther up in the mountains for longer periods due to humans moving in to their habitat, leaving roughly only 700 remaining on Earth.

Amur Leopard

Amur leopard's are native to the Primorye region of southeastern Russia and the Jilin Province of northeast China. They are classified as critically endangered as there are less than 70 remaining in the wild. The poaching of these leopards for their fur is the main threat to their survival.
